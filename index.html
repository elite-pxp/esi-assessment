Scoring FAQ

How is my result calculated?

The assessment produces one primary educator-strength result and identifies the next-highest category as a supporting strength.

  1. Each response receives a value from 1 for Strongly Disagree to 5 for Strongly Agree.
  2. Each strength has eight assigned questions. Those eight values are added to create a category score from 8 to 40.
  3. The category with the highest total becomes your one primary result.
  4. The category ranked second is shown as your supporting strength.
  5. If totals are tied, the assessment compares the number of 5 responses, then 4s, 3s, 2s, and 1s.
  6. If the full response patterns are still identical, a fixed order is used: Relationship Builder, Innovative Educator, Inspirational Leader, Strategic Planner, then Growth Champion.

Strength and color identities

  • Relationship Builder (Blue)
  • Innovative Educator (Green)
  • Inspirational Leader (Red)
  • Strategic Planner (Gold)
  • Growth Champion (Purple)
Only the top-ranked category is your result. The five score cards are shown only as a transparent breakdown of the calculation.
